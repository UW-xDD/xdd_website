GDD Workshop 2018

August 20-21

Pyle Center, UW-Madison, Madison, WI USA

Register now

About

A free hands-on workshop focused on GeoDeepDive infrastructure and current activity in automated interrogation of scholarly publications.

Learn more about GeoDeepDive

Audience

Anyone interested in learning about and applying automated approaches to knowledge and data disovery in the published literature. Whether you are a student, librarian, domain scientist, or technologist, all are welcome regardless of field or technological expertise.

Travel Information

There are several affordable options to get to/from Madison affordably. Flights to MSN are convenient and taxis and ride shares to downtown cost around $15. It is also possible to travel to/from Chicago or Milwaukee and take buses from those airports to UW-Madison.
