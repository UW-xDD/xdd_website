About

GeoDeepDive brings 7.1M scholarly publications and computing capacity within your reach. This free workshop focuses on leveraging new approaches to integrating publications into academic and industry discovery steps and workflows. Workshop supported by NSF C4P.

Learn more about GeoDeepDive

Audience

Anyone interested in learning about or applying algorithmic approaches to knowledge and data discovery and aggregation from published documents. Whether a student, librarian, domain scientist, technologist or industry consultant, all are welcome regardless of field or technical skill level.

Travel Information

There are several affordable options to get to/from Madison, WI. Flights with Delta, United, Frontier, American and Sun Country Airlines are available at MSN. The MSN airport is convenient to campus, with taxis, ride shares and Metro buses connecting to UW-Madison. It is also possible to travel to/from Chicago or Milwaukee and take buses from those airports to UW-Madison.

Support

There is no registration fee for the workshop. Limited financial support for travel/lodging will be provided on an as-needed basis by the C4P NSF EarthCube research coordination network. If you require support to participate, please register and indicate your need in the online form. Support will be prioritized for those those who need it, particularly student and early career participants. You will be notified ASAP about available support.
