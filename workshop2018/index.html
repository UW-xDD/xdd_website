GDD Workshop 2018

August 20-21

Pyle Center, UW-Madison, Madison, WI USA

About

A hands-on workshop focused on GeoDeepDive infrastruture and current activity in text and data mining in scholarly publications.

Who should attend

Anyone interested in learning about and applying automated approaches to knowledge and data disovery in the published literature.
