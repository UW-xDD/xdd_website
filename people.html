UW Project Technical Team

Our team combines expertise in both geoscience and computer science, helping to ensure that the cyberinfrastructure being developed is secure, scalable and dependable while at the same time satisfying the needs of end users. Researchers in geoscience, including UW-Madison postodocs Jon Husson and Andrew Zaffos, Princeton undergraduate Julia Wilcots, and Carnegie Institute postdoc Chao Liu, are helping test the infrastructure and put it to use by developing working applications.
